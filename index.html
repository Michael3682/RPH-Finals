About Catarman

Poblacion is a barangay in the municipality of Cordova, in the province of Cebu. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 10,163. This represented 14.40% of the total population of Cordova. Residents congregate in Baranggay Poblacion for a variety of events, and guests can get a taste of the customs and culture of the area. Barangay Poblacion is probably the town center or central district of Cordova, housing a number of public buildings, businesses, and government offices.
